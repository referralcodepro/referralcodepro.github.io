Understanding MiCA Regulation In Europe

The Markets in Crypto-Assets Regulation (MiCA) is the European Union framework designed to create harmonized rules for crypto assets and crypto asset service providers.

Under MiCA, companies providing crypto services in Europe need to meet specific regulatory requirements, including organizational standards, consumer protection measures and compliance obligations.
