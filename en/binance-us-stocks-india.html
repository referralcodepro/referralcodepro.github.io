What Are Fractional Shares?

Fractional shares allow investors to own a portion of a company instead of purchasing a complete share. This can make some expensive stocks more accessible for beginners.

Example

Instead of purchasing one full share of a high-priced stock, investors may be able to buy a smaller portion if fractional investing is supported.

Benefits

  • Lower starting amount
  • Access to expensive companies
  • Easier portfolio diversification

Things To Check

  • Platform availability
  • Fees
  • Trading limitations
